About

What is DTU Chip Day?

The Event

DTU Chip Day is a yearly half-day conference and exhibition organised by DTU and the semiconductor industry in the Copenhagen area. The event features talks where DTU and industry show how integrated electronics has become the fundamental technology of our time.

Who Should Attend

Students, faculty, and industry professionals interested in the Danish chip design ecosystem. Expect exciting talks, company exhibitions, networking, and opportunities for thesis projects and positions.

Venue

DTU Lyngby Campus, Building 101
